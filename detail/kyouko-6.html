Part-Time At The Adult Shop

Hなお店でアルバイト編 , Hentai na Omise de Arbeit Hen
1.029 Votes

As punishment for her defeat in the neighborhood association, Kyouko-san must work temporarily at a secret adult shop known only by word of mouth among the neighborhood association, a place where housewives "take care" of male customers. Kyouko-san, the new member must serve everyone from the store manager, gardener, schoolchildren, and even her former school teacher.
